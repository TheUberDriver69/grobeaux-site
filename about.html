About

Why Growbeaux exists.

Most weight loss programs are either useless ($60/mo for a meal plan an LLM could write) or predatory (12-week packages, autorenewing fees, advice that breaks you down to sell you the fix). Growbeaux is built differently because we're tired of watching men get fleeced for trying to take care of themselves.

The simple version.

$3 a month gets you a real protocol, a tracker, and a community of men actually doing the work. That's it. Supplements are available if you want them — at member pricing — but they're optional. The program works without them.

We make money on supplements. We don't make money tricking people into renewals.

The whole industry is built on selling fixes that don't work, then selling another one when the first one fails. We're not interested in that game.

What we won't do.

No 12-week packages

Cancel any time, in one tap. If we're earning $3, we should be earning it monthly.

Third-party tested

Every supplement batch is tested by independent labs for purity, potency, and contaminants. Certificates of analysis available on request. No proprietary blends, no fillers, no marketing dust.

No fake transformations

The "before/after" photos you'll see are real members or no photos at all. We'd rather be slow than dishonest.

No selling your data

Your weight, your check-ins, your photos — they stay yours. We don't sell to advertisers, brokers, or anyone.

Who's behind it.

Growbeaux is built by college athletes from Maryland — guys who played baseball at Towson and football at Susquehanna.

We've spent years inside locker rooms, weight rooms, and team meal halls. We've watched friends crash diet through preseason. We've seen guys lose weight too fast and lose the muscle they spent four years building. We've been the guys who cut wrong and looked smaller, not leaner.

Most weight loss programs are built by marketers who never had to make weight, never had to perform, never had to actually keep the body they were trying to change. That gap is why this exists.

We're not influencers. We're not promising a six-pack in twelve weeks. We're building the program we wished we had when we were 22 and trying to figure it out. Real protocols. Real accountability. Real men doing the work alongside you in The Group.

If you're trying to lose the weight without losing yourself — we built this for you.
