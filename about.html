About

Why Growbeaux exists.

Most weight loss programs are either useless ($60/mo for a meal plan an LLM could write) or predatory (12-week packages, autorenewing fees, advice that breaks you down to sell you the fix). Growbeaux is built differently because we're tired of watching men get fleeced for trying to take care of themselves.

The simple version.

$3 a month gets you a real protocol, a tracker, and a community of men actually doing the work. That's it. Supplements and prescription peptides are available if you want them — at member pricing — but they're optional. The program works without them.

We make money on supplements and Rx. We don't make money tricking people into renewals.

The whole industry is built on selling fixes that don't work, then selling another one when the first one fails. We're not interested in that game.

What we won't do.

01

No 12-week packages

Cancel any time, in one tap. If we're earning $3, we should be earning it monthly.

02

No research peptides

Every prescription comes from a licensed physician and a 503A compounding pharmacy. If it's not a real Rx, we don't ship it.

03

No fake transformations

The "before/after" photos you'll see are real members or no photos at all. We'd rather be slow than dishonest.

04

No selling your data

Your weight, your check-ins, your photos — they stay yours. We don't sell to advertisers, brokers, or anyone.

Who's behind it.

Growbeaux is built by a small team in the U.S. — operators, not influencers. We work with licensed physicians, FDA-registered compounding pharmacies, and supplement manufacturers we've vetted ourselves. The names go on the about page once we have something to show.
